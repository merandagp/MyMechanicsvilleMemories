An Auto-Ethnographic Digital History Story by Meranda Gallupe-Paton

This story uses text, photos, videos, and audio. To get the full immersive effect of the story, it is recommended you listen with headphones. Sit an listen for a bit before scrolling.

The Place

This project focus on the history and place memories of Mechanicsville, a once working-class neighbourhood in Ottawa currently in the throes of gentrification. This is not the Mechanicsville in my memories. 


A place that mainly now exists in memory, not many photos of what I will be describing so I'll show what is there now and tell you what I remember.
