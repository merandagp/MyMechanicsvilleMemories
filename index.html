This story uses text, photos, videos, and audio. To get the full immersive effect of the story, it is recommended you listen with headphones. Sit and﻿﻿ listen for a bit before scrolling.

The Place

What you just saw and heard, is similar to what you might see and hear if you visited Mechanicsville today. The handful of blocks on the North West side of Ottawa was once home to the city's railway mechanics, mill hands, and labourers, earning itself the apt name; village of mechanics. 


Like any neighbourhood, Mechanicsville has seen its fair share of change throughout its many years. The Houses embraced families as work came and went. The Streets stood watch as technology changed and railway mechanics turned into auto mechanics. The Sidewalks supported the spirited steps of children as they grew into the wiser, wearier walks of adults. 


The Mechanicsville I know, is a place that mainly exists in memory now...

The Place (Revisited)

What you just saw and heard, is similar to what you might now see and hear if you visited Mechanicsville. The handful of blocks on the North West side of Ottawa is named for its history and is home to those that live and have lived there.


Like any neighbourhood, Mechanicsville will see its fair share of change throughout the years. The Houses will embrace families as work comes and goes. The Streets stand watch as technology continues to evolve. The Sidewalks support the spirited steps of children as they grow into the wiser, wearier walks of adults. 


The Mechanicsville I know, is a memory.
